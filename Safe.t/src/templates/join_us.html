We can't do this alone

As war against COVID rages on and India awaits the next winning by maintaining social distancing, many professionals are asking how they can help. In response, we are setting up a holistic platform for COVID tracking, a new program designed to serve the hospitals, goverment and public.

Our healthcare system has shown extraordinary heroism and resilience in the face of this unprecendented situation. But the uncertainity and imprecise data they live under are causing serious problems in the ecosystem.

To battle this communication gap, we are establishing a one-stop web and mobile platform. This not-for-profit initiative can't be sustained without the support from skilled volunteers, who can push this mission forward.

If you wish to volunteer and help us in any way you can click the link below!
