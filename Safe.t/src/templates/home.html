How It Works

1.

Voluntary symptom check and self-report

We rely on our community to check on our health and anonymously report if we have been tested positive. We then leave it to the experts to use the epidemiological data to scale massive research.

2.

Use the hotspot map to plan your excursion

Sometimes we have to go out! That’s where Safe.t can give you the info to be safe while doing so. You can plan your route to your nearest grocery store by checking which areas to avoid on the Safe.t app!

3.

Risk and Exposure Alerts

Accidentally stepped into a high-risk exposure area? Not to worry! Safe.t will alert youif you have to be cautious! Moreover, if someone in your community has reported positive, there’s an alert for that too after which you can use our “book your test” tab to be sure.

4.

Booking Appointments

Want to book an appointment with your doctor? But afraid to go to an overcrowding hospital? Don’t worry! Just book it from our app in the matter of second!
